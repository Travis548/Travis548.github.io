Degree Pathways - Oregon Institute of Technology

  • The Oregon Institute of Technology is a public polytechnic university based in Klamath Falls, Oregon.
  • They offer Undergraduate options related to the CS Major, such as Computer Engineering Technology and Software Engineering Technology.
  • It does not appear that OIT offers much for Software Development or Computer Engineering at the Graduate level, but has a Concurrent Degree program that allows students to take courses for Software Development and Computer Engineering at the same time.
  • The Bachelor's Degree for Software Development Technology requires 187 credits to complete.
  • The Bachelor's Degree for Computer Engineering Technology requires 186 credits to complete.
  • OIT says that they are partnered with companies such as SpaceX, Intel, HP and others to provide internships for their students.
  • OIT also claims that the average starting salary of its graduates is roughly $58,000.
