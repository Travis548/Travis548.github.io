Degree Pathways - Oregon State University

  • Oregon State University is a public research university based in Corvallis, Oregon.
  • They offer both Undergraduate and Graduate options for the CS Major and Minor.
  • The Oregon State University - Cascades campus based in Bend, Oregon offers a program centered on Mobile Software Development.
  • The main option for the Undergraduate level is in Computer Systems, however if you choose to do the Graduate program you will find that there is a wide range of topics available including, but not limited to:
    • - Cybersecurity
    - Networking and Computer Systems
    - Programming Languages
    - Computer Science Education.
